OP-06 PSA 10 grading data

Across the full OP-06 Wings of the Captain set, 90.6% of PSA-graded cards received PSA 10, across 36,818 total grades. A higher gem rate means more PSA 10 supply, which usually compresses the premium a graded card holds over a raw NM copy; a lower rate keeps gem examples scarce and the premium wide. That is why chase-card value tracks PSA population and gem rate, not just character popularity.
