OP-13 PSA 10 grading data

OP-13 Carrying on His Will cards achieve PSA 10 (gem mint) roughly 87.3% of the time, across 49,092 PSA-graded cards. A higher gem rate means more PSA 10 supply, which usually compresses the premium a graded card holds over a raw NM copy; a lower rate keeps gem examples scarce and the premium wide. That is why chase-card value tracks PSA population and gem rate, not just character popularity.
