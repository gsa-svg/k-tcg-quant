OP-07 PSA 10 grading data

OP-07 500 Years in the Future cards achieve PSA 10 (gem mint) roughly 90% of the time, across 29,680 PSA-graded cards. A higher gem rate means more PSA 10 supply, which usually compresses the premium a graded card holds over a raw NM copy; a lower rate keeps gem examples scarce and the premium wide. That is why chase-card value tracks PSA population and gem rate, not just character popularity. Before you buy a sealed OP-07 box

  • Compare the asking price against recent sold prices, not just listings — active prices are often above what boxes actually sell for.
  • Check the seller's history, photos of the actual box and shrink-wrap condition to reduce reseal risk.
  • Japanese and English boxes price very differently — see Japanese vs English boxes.
  • Understand what actually moves sealed box prices before treating any box as an investment.
