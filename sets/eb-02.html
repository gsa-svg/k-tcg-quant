Reprints & original price

EB-02 launched at a Japanese MSRP of ¥5,280 per 24-pack box (about $32). At today's market price that is roughly 3.4x its original retail. On reprints: Bandai does not publish per-set reprint announcements for the One Piece Card Game, so there is no official count. We found no dated reprint record for EB-02 in our sources — which means none surfaced, not that it was never reprinted. See the full board on the market index page.

EB-02 PSA 10 grading data

EB-02 Anime 25th Collection cards achieve PSA 10 (gem mint) roughly 87% of the time, across 9,922 PSA-graded cards. A higher gem rate means more PSA 10 supply, which usually compresses the premium a graded card holds over a raw NM copy; a lower rate keeps gem examples scarce and the premium wide. That is why chase-card value tracks PSA population and gem rate, not just character popularity.
