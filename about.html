About

Data-driven sealed booster box research

OP Box Index tracks Japanese One Piece sealed booster boxes, active eBay listings, sold-price trends, Top 10 chase cards, NM price references and PSA population data.

The site is built for global collectors in the United States, Japan, Singapore, Malaysia, the Philippines, Thailand, Vietnam, Indonesia and other TCG markets.

What the site measures

  • Booster box active listings and sold-price trend signals.
  • Top 10 chase cards for each set, with NM and PSA 10 references where available.
  • Supply pressure, demand strength, price spread and valuation range indicators.

Operating principles

  • Verified lowest eBay box listings and PSA 10 active links are refreshed every day at 03:00 KST when the automated job can access eBay data.
  • Broader card-price and box-market datasets are refreshed on a weekly cycle, with manual review when matching quality is uncertain.
  • Paid Link: OP Box Index may earn a commission from qualifying purchases made through eBay links, at no extra cost to the buyer.
  • We prefer showing fewer verified links over showing low-quality matches. If a PSA 10 active match is not reliable, the site may show a search link instead of a direct listing.

All information is for research only. It is not financial or investment advice.
