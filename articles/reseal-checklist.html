Checklist

Sealed booster box reseal checklist

Before chasing the lowest price, verify whether the box listing is actually a sealed booster box and whether the photos are enough to judge reseal risk.

Check before buying

  • Front, back, top, bottom and side photos are visible.
  • Wrap pattern, seals and corners match normal factory examples.
  • The title says booster box or display box, not loose packs, live break or pack bundle.
  • The seller has consistent TCG sales history and clear return terms.
  • The total price includes shipping and is not suspiciously below the market range.
