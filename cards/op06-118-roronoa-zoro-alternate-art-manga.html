Raw vs PSA 10: is grading worth it here?

Right now a PSA 10 sells at roughly the same price as — or below — a raw NM copy. At today's numbers, grading this card adds fees, shipping and months of turnaround for no price upside. That happens with high-supply modern cards: when 2,541 PSA 10s already exist, the slab premium collapses.
