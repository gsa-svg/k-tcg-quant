How much is Boa Hancock SP (EB03-026) worth?

As of 2026-07-22, the raw Japanese near-mint copy runs about ¥248,000 ($1,526) at Japanese retail, and PSA 10 copies have sold near $2,018. Prices move with the market; the figures on this page update with our data refreshes.

How rare is a PSA 10 of this card?
